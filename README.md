# 🏁 In Lap Evaluations - Public Support Desk

Welcome to the public support tracker for **In Lap Evaluations**, built by Chicane Works LLC. 

This repository serves as the central hub for tracking bug reports, support tickets, and feature requests. By keeping these tickets public, we ensure transparency and allow you to track the exact status of your issue in real time.

---

### 🏎️ How to Get Help

If you are running into an issue on the platform (like an error submitting a review) or have an idea for a new feature, you don't need a GitHub account to get help! 

**1. Try a "Hard Refresh" First**
Many common web errors happen because your browser is holding onto an old, outdated version of the site. Try clearing it by pressing:
* **Mac:** `Command + Shift + R`
* **Windows:** `Control + Shift + R`

**2. Email Support**
If a refresh doesn't fix it, email support directly at bugreport@inlapevaluations.com. To help us squash the bug as quickly as possible, please copy and paste the following list into your email and fill out as much as you can:

* **What is happening:** (Describe the issue and the exact error message you are seeing. Screenshots are highly encouraged!)
* **What you expected to happen:** (e.g., "I expected the page to redirect back to my dashboard.")
* **Steps to reproduce:** (Walk us through exactly what you clicked or typed right before the problem occurred.)
* **Device:** (e.g., iPhone, Windows PC, Mac)
* **Operating System:** (e.g., iOS, Windows 11)
* **Browser:** (e.g., Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
* *(Optional for savvy users)* **Console Logs:** If you know how to open your browser's developer tools, please paste any errors showing in the Console.

**3. Track Your Ticket**
Once your email is received, a ticket will be created here on your behalf. You will be emailed a direct link to the ticket so you can follow the progress.

---

### 🚦 Understanding Your Ticket Status

When you view a ticket, you will see a status block at the top showing exactly where things stand:

* 🔴 **Blocked:** The issue is preventing core tasks, and fixing it is the top priority.
* 🟡 **Investigating / Under Review:** The issue is actively being looked into or scoped.
* 🟢 **Resolved / Approved:** The bug has been squashed, or the feature is going into the backlog to be built.

---

### ⚠️ Privacy & Security Notice

**This is a PUBLIC repository.** Anyone on the internet can see the issues posted here. 

When communicating about a bug or a feature request, **never include sensitive information**. 
* Do not include the full names, contact information, or specific track telemetry/driving evaluations of your students.
* Do not post passwords or login credentials. 
* If sensitive data is required to troubleshoot your specific issue, it will be handled privately via a secure video call or direct email.
